# Lab 2: PCI DSS Compliance Review

## Overview
Conducted a compliance gap analysis against the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) for a healthcare organization that processes payment card transactions through its HNetPay system. Mapped existing controls to PCI DSS requirements and identified gaps requiring remediation.

## Skills Demonstrated
- Interpreting and applying PCI DSS requirements
- Performing a structured compliance gap analysis
- Prioritizing remediation actions based on risk level
- Documenting compliance findings in a professional format

## Tools Used
- PCI DSS v3.2.1 / v4.0 requirements documentation
- SimpleRisk (risk and compliance documentation)
- Compliance checklist templates

## Key Takeaways
- Identified multiple gaps in the HNetPay environment, including insufficient network segmentation and weak access controls
- Learned that PCI DSS compliance is an ongoing process, not a one-time assessment
- Understood the relationship between technical controls (firewalls, encryption) and procedural controls (policies, training)
- Recognized that non-compliance with PCI DSS can result in significant financial penalties and reputational damage

## Relevant Frameworks
- PCI DSS v4.0 — Full standard (12 core requirements)
- NIST SP 800-30 Rev. 1 — Risk Assessment methodology
- NIST CSF — Identify (ID.GV): Governance; Protect (PR.AC): Access Control
